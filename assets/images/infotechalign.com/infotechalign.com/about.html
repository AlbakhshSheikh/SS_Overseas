COMPANY OVERVIEW

AliGn Infotech is a multicity Software Electronic based organization, based in Pune, Mumbai, Nashik , Nagpur and Ahmednagar, specializing across various arenas of the IT, Electronic and the digital marketing industry. AliGn Infotech has skilled the craft of customized web development, development of distributed and enterprise-wide robust applications, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), PPC, content management, hardware based product, App store optimization (ASO), Digital or Internet marketing and high quality visual designs using the updated tools and technologies The unique commingle of in-depth domain understanding and insight with cutting edge technical competence gives AliGn Infotech an upper hand to high quality services to our clients across the globe for the last 4 years.

The competency of AliGn Infotech has expanded within a short span of time in some of the new and areas of technology like Business Intelligence, Internet of Things (IOT), Artificial Intelligence, Image processing and Machine Learning Services around these new edge technologies help businesses gain high quality, high accuracy, highly scalable and cost effective digital and hardware solutions.

Besides these one of our differentiator is ‘Hire a Dedicated Resource/ Virtual Assistant’ program which is an unique way for clients to avail our services in the form of a devoted resource to their Web App development, Web designing, Content Writing and Internet Marketing requirements. This service offers a cost-effect solution to prospective clients around the globe who do not wish to invest in a full-time resource for the same tasks.
