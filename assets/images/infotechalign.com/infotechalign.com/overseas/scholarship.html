SCHOLARSHIP


STUDY-IN-ITALY
SCHOLARSHIP



Studying overseas may lead to high expenses and can elevate your temperatures. As a student, you will always think of more cost saving and efficient way because you don’t want to jeopardize your parent’s life. To save you from being a headache to your parents, a concept of Scholarship makes national as well as overseas education accessible, sustainable and acceptable. Like every nation has some specific criteria, Italy has some. There are organisation which work for the students and perks of such organisations is to be of some help to a student in terms of finance for studies, basically this organisations are in charge of every scholarships available for students worldwide.


There are various scholarships offered to international students, some of these even cover 100% academic fees and accommodation facilities. Italian government promotes education in Italy by providing scholarships for foreign citizens and Italian citizens residing abroad (IRE). Majorly all the Master Programs are covered under scholarships apart from them Higher education in Arts, Music, language, cultural courses, and dance, Ph.D. programs, and other research programs are also covered under the scholarships.

The Italian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Italian Ministry for Economic Development, has launched a program “Invest your talent in Italy” offering scholarships for Master’s and postgraduate degree courses in various disciplines.

This scholarship is awarded to students hailing from India, Azerbaijan, Egypt, Kazakhstan, Colombia, Ethiopia, Ghana, Indonesia, Mexico, Iran, Turkey, Tunisia, and Vietnam. Some of the organisations making your idea of studying overseas successful are as follows:



EDISU Piemonte Scholarships :

The students of Undergraduate courses, Post graduate courses and PhD degree courses of Universities of Piedmont can apply for these scholarship. It is also applicable for part-time students if they enroll in bachelor degree. The amount varies and accommodation can also be provided to non-resident students.

Bologna University Study Grants for International Students:

This university offers scholarship of one academic year to Bachelor degree, Masters degree and single cycle degree programs with waiver in complete tuition fee and amount of €11059

University of Pavia International Scholarships for Developing Country Students :

This scholarship of €8000 is offered to deserving students of developing countries for Master’s degree program.

Politechnico di Torino International Scholarships:

This scholarship is dedicated for students of Engineering and Architecture. UCSC international scholarship offer around 30% discount in tuition fee.

Lazio disu Scholarship:

This scholarship is offered on the basis of competition and covers the cost of retention. It is offered on the basis of income status of the student.

University of Padova Scholarships for All Degree Courses:

This scholarship is offered to low-income group students offering partly money and partly services such as accommodation/meals at university canteens.

Scuola Normale Superiore PhD Scholarships:

This scholarship is offered to 78 PhD students with complete financial support including tuition fees and cost of living scholarship.

Bocconi Merit and International Awards:

It is offered with amount of €12000 to Bachelor’s program student and €15000 to Masters of Science program students in terms of full or half tuition fee waiver.
